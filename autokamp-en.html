Autocamp

In front of our house, there is a small and quiet autocamp that is shaded from the sun and isolated from the road, providing nice accommodation in the pine and olive trees shade only 70 meters away from the sea and 500 meters from the center of Turanj with main beach.

Camp surface is around 150 m2 southern from the house, plus toiletes and bathrooms which are placed on the north side of the house (providing visual barrier). We also offer all-year use of the camp, so that our guests can pick their favourite spot and leave their camper over winter for us to keep.

 

Autocamp plan and equipment

Autocamp has bathroom part with laundry washers and toilets, placed on the north side of the house, providing privacy and visual barrier from the camp. Equipment fulfills all safety standards, so our guests can count on pleasant accommodation.
